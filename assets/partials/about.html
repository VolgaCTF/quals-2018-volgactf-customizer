VolgaCTF 2018 Quals regulations

Greetings everybody and welcome to VolgaCTF 2018 Quals!

To take part in the competition, you should sign up with an email address, your team's name (Latin characters only) and a logo. Warning! You will not be able to change your team's name once registered so please be careful in order not to take other team's name by accident. The number of team members is not limited.

Please specify a valid email address to get a verification email. It will be impossible to change the email address after you confirm your participation by clicking the link in the verification email.

Besides, we kindly ask you to specify your country in the registration form - we are collecting this information for statistical purposes.

Teams from Russia are also asked to fill in an institution field.

The contest starts on March 24 at 19:00 SAMT/15:00 UTC and lasts 48 hours. Flags match /VolgaCTF{[\x20-\x7F]+}/ unless stated otherwise. Check the News page at times - there might be some important pieces of information about the competition. We will answer your questions and make other announcements in Telegram public chat. Technical support is also provided by email quals2017@volgactf.ru.

Competitors are forbidden to perform destructive actions against competition infrastructure (*.volgactf.ru and *.volgactf.org) and to prevent other teams from playing. A team violating the rules will be disqualified.

While conversing in Telegram public chat, you are expected to be respectful to each other and to restrain from using swear words. Exchanging flags is strictly prohibited. A person asking for, or trading their flags will be expelled from the chat.

We reserve the right to amend these regulations at any time.

VolgaCTF 2017 Finals regulations

VolgaCTF 2017 Finals (attack-defence) will be held on September 11-15 in Samara, Russia.

In 2017 we have the following quotas for VolgaCTF 2017 Finals:

  • 5 teams from the Volga Federal District of Russia,
  • 5 teams from other regions of Russia,
  • 3 foreign teams.

We are going to invite top teams (according to VolgaCTF 2017 Quals scoreboard) on the condition that these teams have specified institution of higher education in their profile. If there appears to be several teams coming from the same educational establishment in the list, we will invite the one having the highest rank among those teams.

The board may change these quotas on technical grounds. Also, we may invite up to 3 guest teams by means of additional quota.

Invitations will be sent to emails specified in teamsâ€™ profiles. It is crucial that the team eligible for participation in VolgaCTF 2017 Finals confirms its participation by Aug 1.

The preliminary list of the teams invited to VolgaCTF 2017 Finals will be published on the competition website no later than 14 days after VolgaCTF 2017 Quals is finished.

Please note that a team will be excluded from this list:

  • if a team denies taking part in the competition, or does not provide timely confirmation of participation,
  • if a team is unable to cover its travel and accommodation costs (we do not provide financial support),
  • if more than half of members in a team graduated more than 2 years ago.

Should any team be excluded from the preliminary list, the next one of the same quota (according to VolgaCTF 2017 Quals scoreboard) will be invited.

Last but not least, we have a limit to the number of participants per team, that is up to 7 members in each team.

We reserve the right to amend these regulations at any time.
